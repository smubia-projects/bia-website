Neural-Linguistics for FinTech

A real-time Natural Language Processing dashboard designed to identify market signals from unstructured financial news streams.

Project Overview

Our Rationale

Financial markets are increasingly driven by sentiment found in alternative data sources. However, traditional tools often fail to capture the nuance of industry-specific jargon. This project was initiated to bridge the gap between raw web-scraped financial news and actionable quantitative data.

By leveraging fine-tuned BERT models specifically trained on financial corpora, we achieved a 14% improvement in entity recognition accuracy compared to off-the-shelf models. The architecture utilizes a serverless pipeline to process over 500 news articles per minute, feeding into the custom visualization layer seen above.

Project Satisfaction

Team Satisfaction: 4.5/5. We are highly satisfied with the technical architecture and the accuracy of the model, though we feel there is room for further optimization in the real-time processing latency.

lightbulb

Key Takeaway

Moving forward, we identified that the latency in real-time streaming could be further optimized by shifting from REST to WebSockets for the final visualization layer. This would provide a more fluid user experience during peak market volatility hours.
